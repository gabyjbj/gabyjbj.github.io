About Rotterdam

Rotterdam is a city in the Netherlands, in South Holland. Its history goes back to 1270, when a dam was constructed in the Rotte river, after which people settled around it for safety. During WWII German Army invaded the Netherlands and the heart of Rotterdam was almost completely destroyed. Rotterdam was gradually rebuild after the war.
